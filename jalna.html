Jalna District

Humidity

Average: 50-55%

Range: 25% in summer to 70% during monsoon

Rainfall

Annual: 700-800 mm

Monsoon: June to September

Soil

Types: Black soil, Red soil

pH: 7.0-7.5

Harvesting Seasons

Kharif: September-October

Rabi: February-March

Major Crops

Jowar

Main food crop, drought-resistant

Cotton

Cash crop, grown in black soil

Soybean

Oilseed crop, grown in kharif season

Pulses

Food crops, grown in both seasons

Wheat

Food crop, grown in rabi season
