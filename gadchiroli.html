Gadchiroli District

Humidity

Average: 65-70%

Range: 40% in summer to 85% during monsoon

Rainfall

Annual: 1000-1100 mm

Monsoon: June to September

Soil

Types: Red soil, Laterite soil

pH: 6.0-6.5

Harvesting Seasons

Kharif: September-October

Rabi: February-March

Major Crops

Rice

Main food crop, grown in irrigated areas

Turmeric

Spice crop, grown in irrigated areas

Soybean

Oilseed crop, grown in kharif season

Pulses

Food crops, grown in both seasons

Maize

Food crop, grown in kharif season
