About Maharashtra Agriculture

This project aims to provide comprehensive agricultural information for all districts in Maharashtra. Our interactive map allows users to explore detailed data about crops, climate conditions, and agricultural practices specific to each district.

The information presented here is carefully curated to help farmers, researchers, and anyone interested in Maharashtra's agriculture sector make informed decisions.
