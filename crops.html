Major Crops of Maharashtra

Jowar (Sorghum)

Staple food crop of Maharashtra, drought-resistant and grown in both kharif and rabi seasons.

Sugarcane

Major cash crop grown in irrigated areas, particularly in western Maharashtra.

Groundnut

Important oilseed crop grown in kharif season, particularly in Marathwada region.

Pomegranate

Fruit crop grown in irrigated areas, known for its quality and export value.

Onion

Major vegetable crop, Maharashtra is one of the largest producers in India.

Cotton

Important cash crop grown in Vidarbha region, known for its quality fiber.

Grapes

Major fruit crop in Nashik region, known as the 'Grape Capital of India'.

Rice

Staple food crop grown in Konkan region, particularly in areas with high rainfall.

Soybean

Important oilseed crop grown in kharif season, particularly in Vidarbha region.

Tomato

Major vegetable crop grown in irrigated areas, particularly in western Maharashtra.

Coconut

Major plantation crop in Konkan region, grown in coastal areas with high humidity.

Arecanut

Important plantation crop in Konkan region, grown in high rainfall areas.

Cashew

Major plantation crop in Konkan region, Maharashtra is a leading producer in India.

Mango

Important fruit crop, particularly in Konkan and western Maharashtra regions.

Wheat

Major rabi season crop grown in irrigated areas of western Maharashtra.

Pulses

Important food crops including gram, tur, and moong, grown in both seasons.

Maize

Important cereal crop grown in kharif season, particularly in Vidarbha region.

Turmeric

Important spice crop grown in western Maharashtra and Marathwada regions.

Banana

Major fruit crop grown in irrigated areas of western Maharashtra.

Orange

Important fruit crop in Vidarbha region, particularly in Nagpur district.

Chickpea (Chana)

Important pulse crop grown in rabi season, particularly in Marathwada and Vidarbha regions.

Sunflower

Important oilseed crop grown in rabi season, particularly in western Maharashtra.

Safflower

Traditional oilseed crop grown in rabi season, particularly in Marathwada region.

Custard Apple (Sitaphal)

Important fruit crop grown in Konkan and western Maharashtra regions.

Guava

Major fruit crop grown in various regions of Maharashtra, known for its quality.
