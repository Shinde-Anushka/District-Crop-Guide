Chandrapur District

Humidity

Average: 60-65%

Range: 35% in summer to 80% during monsoon

Rainfall

Annual: 900-1000 mm

Monsoon: June to September

Soil

Types: Black soil, Red soil

pH: 6.5-7.5

Harvesting Seasons

Kharif: September-October

Rabi: February-March

Major Crops

Cotton

Main cash crop, grown in black soil

Soybean

Oilseed crop, grown in kharif season

Jowar

Staple food crop, drought-resistant

Turmeric

Spice crop, grown in irrigated areas

Pulses

Food crops, grown in both seasons
